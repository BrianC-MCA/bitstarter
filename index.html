constrvct.com has introduced an exciting new 'fashion design revolution', introducing bespoke fashion design and custom manufacture of garments. You can provide your body measurements and they will automatically create a pattern that will match your measurements. However, there is currently no way to provide many of the subtle contour measurements that are needed for a 'tailored' fit, and no way to visualize what you will look like in any of the online garments until you actually receive it in the mail. This is all about to change...

Using the most basic of tools - your own iPhone / iPad camera and a plain paper printer - the Webequin app will allow you to create an exact set of body measurements and an accurate 3D 'mannequin' of yourself. It works like this: you first print out a special QR code on a plain sheet of 8.5" x 11" paper and tape it to your back. You then place your cellphone with the camera pointing towards a spot roughly 8' away and start the Webequin app. Press the app's capture button, walk to where the camera is pointed to and record yourself slowly turning clockwise with your arms outstreched (remembering to smile!). When you're done, hit the stop button and the app will auto-generate an accurate (though tastefully modest) 3D avatar of you, complete with an XML file with all of your basic measurements. You can then review the measurements it has calculated and modify any that appear to be incorrect. The app will then generate supplemental measurements to provide a precise fit, and will upload your avatar and the measurements file to the Webequin web site. You'll then be able to access this info from fashion websites that support the Webequin data file format and view how the digital garments fit online from all angles.

All of this is enabled by a) the known dimensions of the QR code on the sheet of paper, b) a lot of math, and c) providing the web-based WebGL software for digital fashion houses to use the measurements and avatar. To monetize the app, a couple of cents will be charged to the fashion design house for each sale that uses Webequin's data file - to the customer it's totally free.

Other applications include 3D games, SecondLife, etc.
